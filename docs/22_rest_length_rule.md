# Rest Length Rule

Relaxing videos do not require creating 8 hours of original music.

## Base Rule

- Create a 50-70 minute Master Playlist.
- Loop the Master Playlist naturally to reach the target duration.

## User Target Duration Options

- 2 hours
- 3 hours
- 4 hours
- 8 hours
- 10 hours
- Custom

## Internal Logic

- Automatically calculate the required repeat count.
- Do not cut off the final video in the middle of a song.
- End the final video at the end of a playlist cycle.

## Rules

- The Master Playlist should be long enough to avoid obvious repetition.
- Looping should feel natural and non-disruptive.
- Target duration is a goal, but clean musical endings are preferred.
- If ending at a playlist boundary exceeds the target duration, Creator OS should show the final estimated duration.
