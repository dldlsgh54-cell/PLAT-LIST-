# Originality System

Creator OS does not use Gemini alone to judge plagiarism.

Creator OS does not provide external plagiarism judgment.

Instead, it performs strong internal similarity checks to reduce repetition and originality risk inside the user's own library and projects.

## Features

- Music Fingerprint
- Chroma / MFCC similarity
- Lyrics Similarity
- Thumbnail Similarity
- Ambient Fingerprint
- Playlist DNA
- Originality Score

## Rules

- Gemini cannot make final plagiarism decisions.
- Creator OS does not claim legal plagiarism detection.
- Similarity checks are internal to the user's library, projects, and generated assets.
- If a similar song is found, Creator OS recommends replacement or regeneration.
- High similarity risk should be shown before approval and rendering.
- Originality Score should contribute to CQI.

## Purpose

- Avoid repeating the user's own prior songs too closely.
- Reduce duplicate-feeling playlists.
- Identify similar lyrics, images, thumbnails, and ambient assets.
- Support better album diversity and brand freshness.
