# Vocal Playlist Rule

Vocal playlists are managed by total duration, not by track count.

## Duration Targets

- Minimum: 60 minutes
- Recommended: 60-75 minutes
- If over 90 minutes: recommend creating Part 2

## Selection Priority

1. Music quality
2. Album flow
3. Variety
4. Originality and similarity control
5. Total playback duration

## Rules

- Do not force long tracks into the playlist just to reach the target duration.
- Exclude low-quality tracks even when they are long.
- Playlist duration matters, but quality comes first.
- The selected playlist must be at least 60 minutes before rendering.
- When the project exceeds 90 minutes, Creator OS should suggest splitting into Part 2.
- Similar tracks should be managed carefully to avoid a repetitive playlist.
